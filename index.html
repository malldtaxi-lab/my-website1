Replace the Logo

Click the logo box in the hero and swap it with your own image. Aim for a transparent PNG or SVG for best results.

  • Recommended size around 220x220px.
  • Keep background transparent if possible.
  • Set alt text to your brand name for accessibility.

Edit Your Slogan

Update the headline and subtext in the hero to reflect your brand promise. Keep it concise and benefit-focused.

  • Headline: 3–6 impactful words.
  • Subtext: 1–2 short sentences explaining value.
  • Use action-oriented language.

Optional Visuals

Use clean, on-brand images. These can be removed if you prefer a minimal page.
